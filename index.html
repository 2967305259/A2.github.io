📩 Wang Lu's Letter

🔹 Para.1 After I attended your summer camp about health and lifestyle choices, I realised I hardly ever felt well, either physically or mentally. I often felt sleepy and dizzy, and lacked passion. Most worrying, though, I got the flu easily and experienced many toothaches, too.
🔹 Para.2 At the end of the camp I heard, “Change the world by changing yourself.” This stimulated my motivation. A dentist once examined me and told me that too much sugar in my meals had damaged my teeth and health. I made up my mind to change two things in my life: to eat nothing with sugar and to exercise regularly.
🔹 Para.3-4 After that I stopped buying all the sweets, biscuits, and sugary drinks... Whenever I went out with friends, I brought snacks from home: fruit, nuts, dried meat, etc.

My exercise was very simple: I went outside every day for at least 30 minutes and did something active. Sometimes I played table tennis... Other times I jumped on my skateboard...
🔹 Para.5 The results have been fantastic. I feel more dynamic and stronger than ever... I no longer suffer from a flu virus or toothaches either. Best of all... I feel like I’m in control of my own life. I CAN change myself.
Yours truly, Wang Lu

📩 George Fielding's Letter

🔸 Para.1-2 As I took part in the summer camp... I started to analyse my choices... After starting high school, I felt stressed out and always relaxed by playing computer games. I got so absorbed that I played the games day and night. Playing was fun, but I felt even more worn out afterwards!
🔸 Para.2 So after the camp, I realised that it was very, very important to adjust my lifestyle. I decided to take control of my life and find other ways to relax.
🔸 Para.3 It was a big struggle not to join my friends in playing online games... However, I realised that to succeed in quitting, I had to replace it with something else. Rock climbing, bowling, watching comedies, and playing basketball were the things I thought of doing instead.
🔸 Para.4-5 Now my dad and I go rock climbing together monthly! It has enhanced the quality of my life, improving my health and increasing my happiness... After six months of trying out new ways of relaxing, I feel much more energetic. I feel that I can change myself for the better...
Sincerely, George Fielding
